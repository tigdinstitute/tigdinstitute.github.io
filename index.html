About Us

The Trauma-Informed Graphic Design Institute is the consulting and research home for this work. It was founded by Steve Brackenrich, a designer and researcher whose career spans offset press production, healthcare communications, domestic violence advocacy design, and graduate teaching at Kent State University. His graduate research defined Trauma-Informed Graphic Design as a field designation and introduced the concept of design investment disparity to the literature. The institute partners with clinics, hospitals, and mission-driven organizations to design patient-facing materials that account for cognitive load, emotional state, and the realities of seeking care.
